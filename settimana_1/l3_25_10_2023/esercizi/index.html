Focaccia with Caramelized Onions, Cranberries, and Brie

Wow your teste buds with this festtive focaccia appetizer topped with sweet caramelized onions, tart cranberries, and creamy brie. It will become your favourite holiday starter!

FEATURED IN:
Fresh Cranberries Recipes That Go Beyond Sauce

Ingredients

For the focaccia:

  • 4 cups (500g) all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 teaspoons instant yeast
  • 1/2 teaspoons salt
  • 3/4 cups (350g) lukewarm water
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil, plus more for the pan

For the topping:

  • 3 medium yellow onions, thinly sliced
  • 1 tablespoon balsamic vinegar
  • 1-2 tablespoons olive oil, for brushing on top
  • 10 ounces brie, cut into cubes (scrape rind off with a pairing knife if desired, though it's not necessary)
  • 1 1/2 cups fresh or frozen cranberries

Method

  1. Make the focaccia dough


    In a medium-sized mixing bowl, or in a stand mixer with a paddle attachment, mix togheter all the dough ingredient until the flour is fully absorbed. You don't need to actually knead the dough, tough i sometimes knead it a few times in the bowl when mixing by hand to incorporate the last of the flour...
